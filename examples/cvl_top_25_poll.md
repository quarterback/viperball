## Ranking Methodology

**Ranking Score Formula:**
- Win Percentage: 40%
- Strength of Schedule: 30%
- Viper Efficiency: 20%
- Point Differential: 10%

**Viper Efficiency:** (Total Yards / Downs Used) × (1 + Lateral Success Rate) × Viper Impact Factor

**Strength of Schedule:** Based on opponent win percentage and cross-conference adjustments
