CATLIN GABEL SCHOOL - TENNIS PROGRAM TOP 10 LUMINARIES
=======================================================

GIRLS TOP 10
-------------

1. Lara Rakocevic
   - 4x state singles qualifier (2013-2016)
   - 4x district singles champion (2013-2016)
   - State singles titles
   - Dominated the district for four consecutive years

2. Amanda Perez
   - 3x state qualifier (2024 singles, 2023 doubles, 2025 doubles)
   - District doubles champion (2025) with Jiya Mehta
   - District singles finalist
   - State singles QF
   - Won freshman doubles title (2023) with different partner (Lily Wand)
   - If she wins again in 2026: 3 district doubles titles with 3 different partners

3. Alicia Hunt
   - 4x state singles qualifier (2001-2004)
   - Individual state singles title(s)
   - Anchor of back-to-back state team champions (2002-2003)

4. Kate Rubinstein
   - 4x state singles qualifier (2009-2012)
   - Individual state singles title(s)
   - Team narrowly missed state title (lost by ~1 point)
   - Did everything within her control to win one for the team

5. Allaire Paterson
   - 4x state singles qualifier (1977-1980)
   - State singles semifinalist (1980)
   - Part of 1980 Class AA-A State Team Champions (CG 14, Condon 13)
   - Anchor of the only CG girls team to win a state title

6. Lola Diaz Gonzalez
   - 3x state qualifier (2019, 2021, 2022)
   - 3x district singles champion (2019, 2021, 2022)
   - State singles finalist (2022)
   - Led team to a state title
   - The gold standard of her era

7. Jiya Mehta
   - 3x state qualifier (2023, 2024, 2025 - all doubles)
   - 2x district doubles champion (2024 with Elise Kim, 2025 with Amanda Perez)

8. Gretchen MacColl
   - 3x state doubles qualifier (1994-1996)
   - Part of state doubles championship team
   - Played with multiple partners across years

9. Lily Wand
   - State doubles champion (2022 with Thea Kapteyn)
   - District doubles champion (2022)
   - Top-2 seed at both state tournaments she attended
   - COVID canceled her other 2 eligible state tournament years
   - Only got 2 state tournaments due to pandemic timing
   - Would likely have had 4 state appearances without COVID

10. Lindsey Dickinson
    - 2x state doubles qualifier (1995-1996)
    - Part of state doubles championship team (with Gretchen MacColl)

NOTE ON CLASSIFICATIONS:
Catlin Gabel has historically competed in the small-school classification
(Class AA-A in the 1970s-80s, currently 3A). Current CG players are competitive
at the 6A level individually; the school's 3A classification reflects enrollment
size and lineup depth, not the quality of top players.

NOTE ON COVID-19:
The 2020 season was fully canceled. The 2021 season held district tournaments
but no state tournament. Players active in 2019-2022 had their careers
significantly affected, particularly those who lost 1-2 state tournament
opportunities (e.g., Lily Wand, Rachael Yoken, Anna Semler).
