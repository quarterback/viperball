NOTE ON CLASSIFICATIONS:
Prior to the classification system (roughly pre-1990), Catlin Gabel competed
against all Oregon schools regardless of size. Making state in that era meant
beating players from the largest 6A programs. Current CG players are competitive
at the 6A level individually; the school's 3A classification reflects enrollment
size and lineup depth, not the quality of top players.

NOTE ON COVID-19:
The 2020 season was fully canceled. The 2021 season held district tournaments
but no state tournament. Players active in 2019-2022 had their careers
significantly affected, particularly those who lost 1-2 state tournament
opportunities (e.g., Lily Wand, Rachael Yoken, Anna Semler).
