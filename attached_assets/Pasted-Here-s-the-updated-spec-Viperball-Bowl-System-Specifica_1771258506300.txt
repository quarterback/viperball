# Viperball Bowl System Specification

## Overview

The postseason consists of two components: a **configurable playoff** (4, 8, 12, or 16 teams) for the championship and a **bowl system** for remaining eligible teams. Bowls reward regular season success and ensure competitive teams get a postseason game even if they miss the playoff.

---

## Playoff and Bowl Relationship

The playoff and bowl system are separate but connected:

1. **Playoff teams are selected first** (top 4, 8, 12, or 16 teams)
2. **Playoff teams are excluded from bowls** â€” they're competing for the championship
3. **Remaining eligible teams fill the bowl pool**

This means larger playoffs leave fewer teams for bowls, and smaller playoffs create a deeper bowl field.

---

## Bowl Eligibility

### Primary Rule
Teams with a **.500 or better record** are bowl-eligible.

| Regular Season Games | Bowl-Eligible Record |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| 8 games | 4-4 or better |
| 10 games | 5-5 or better |
| 12 games | 6-6 or better |

### Pool Filling Rule
If there aren't enough .500+ teams to fill all bowl slots after removing playoff teams, add sub-.500 teams in order of record until all bowls are filled.

**Example:** 6 bowls (12 teams needed), 8-team playoff removes top 8
- 10 remaining teams are .500+
- Add the 2 best sub-.500 teams to reach 12

### Minimum Threshold
If even after adding sub-.500 teams there aren't enough to fill bowls, reduce bowl count to match available teams. A bowl requires 2 teams.

## Bowl Tiering and Matchup Logic

### Core Principle
**Bowls match similar teams together.** An 11-1 team should not play a 5-7 team.

### Matchup Algorithm

```
1. Select playoff teams (4, 8, 12, or 16)
2. Remove playoff teams from bowl pool
3. Rank remaining teams by:
   a. Win percentage (primary)
   b. Point differential (tiebreaker)
   c. Head-to-head if applicable
4. Check eligibility:
   a. Count .500+ teams
   b. If count < (bowl_count Ã— 2), add best sub-.500 teams
5. Pair teams by rank:
   - Bowl 1 (Tier 1): #1 vs #2
   - Bowl 2 (Tier 1): #3 vs #4
   - Bowl 3 (Tier 2): #5 vs #6
   - Continue until all bowls filled
6. Optional: Apply conference balancing (avoid same-conference matchups)
```

### Matchup Quality Guardrails

| Rule | Implementation |
|------|----------------|
| Max win differential | 3 games (e.g., 9-3 can play 7-5, but not 5-7) |
| Same conference | Avoid if possible; swap with next-closest team |
| Regular season rematch | Allowed but deprioritized |

If guardrails can't be satisfied (small league, limited pool), override and create best available matchup.

### Tiering

| Tier | Bowl Position | Typical Records |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Tier 1 (Premier) | Bowls 1â€“2 | 8+ wins |
| Tier 2 (Major) | Bowls 3â€“4 | 6â€“8 wins |
| Tier 3 (Standard) | Bowls 5+ | 5â€“7 wins |

## Edge Cases

| Scenario | Handling |
|----------|----------|
| League size â‰¤ playoff size | No bowls possible; playoff only |
| All remaining teams sub-.500 | Fill bowls with best available; tier as "Consolation" |
| Odd number of eligible teams | Last team out misses bowl (or add one more sub-.500) |
| Not enough teams for configured bowls | Reduce bowl count automatically; notify user |
| 16-team playoff in 20-team league | Only 4 teams remain; max 2 bowls possible |
| User sets 0 bowls | Valid; playoff only, no bowl games |
